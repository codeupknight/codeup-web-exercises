Create a new html file under codeup.dev/public called "ajax_blog.html". At minimum, your ajax blog will need an empty
with id="posts". Add your own Bootstrap theme to this file as well. Feel free to use the same one as your store or choose a different one. Download blog.json to your data directory from before. Use ajax to load the contents of blog.json and add the data from it to your #posts div. Add additional entries to blog.json and make sure your changes are reflected on the page.
